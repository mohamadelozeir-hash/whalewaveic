About Whale Wave IC

Our Expertise

Looking for a partner to bring your project to life? At Whale Wave IC, we provide reliable and precise IC design and layout services with deep expertise in analog, RF, and mixed-signal domains. Our experienced team works closely with you to deliver high-quality solutions you can trust, helping your project succeed from concept to completion.

Our Services

Digital/ Analog TX

Transmitters are among the most power-hungry blocks in any RF system. The traditional approach is the Analog Transmitter, and at Whale Wave we have extensive experience designing these architectures end-to-end. Whether you need a Mixer, Power Amplifier, or Phase Shifter, we support the entire flow—from concept development, to circuit design, layout, and post-layout verification.

A more modern approach is the Digital Transmitter, often referred to as an RF DAC. We also have strong expertise in this domain and can guide you in adopting this technology to overcome the limitations of analog circuits and achieve state-of-the-art performance, with full support across concept, design, layout, and post-layout verification.

Power Management

Power-management units are essential building blocks in any low-power system. At WhaleWave, we provide complete support across the entire development flow—from concept definition to circuit design, layout, and post-layout verification. Our expertise covers key IPs such as LDOs, Bias Generators, Current DACs, and a wide range of supporting circuits.

If you have a specific requirement in mind, we're here to help bring it from concept to silicon.

Passive Structures

Passive structures are key to saving area in RF designs. Transmission lines occupy significant silicon area, so smart passive design can reduce both footprint and cost. We can help you design Transformers, Couplers, Inductors, Power Combiners, and Splitters.

BIST

Simulation results often look great under typical conditions — but have you tried running worst-case corner simulations or Monte Carlo simulations? Design not working? Results much worse than expected? We're here to help.

From concept development to design, layout, and post-layout verification, we support the full flow. Whether you need a Phase Detector, Amplitude Detector, Tunable Attenuator, or another custom block, we're here to support you.
